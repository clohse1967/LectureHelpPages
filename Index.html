Chemistry Practice Problems - Ms. Lohse's Chemistry Class

This is the entry point to a set of pages aimed at aiding you in your studies of chemistry. There are currently 105 pages available. The pages are of three types:
1. Drill and Practice-generate random problems in a table format for you.
2. Word Problems-present random word problems in specifc areas.
3. Data Analysis-require that you provide some imput data for the page to use.

The pages are sorted by general categories and are most easily reached by using the first table below to reach a category. You may also scroll down this page to the full list of pages. There is now a fairly comprehensive treatment of stoichiometry and of the uses of chemical formulas. After the page title, you will find (DP) if it is a Drill and Practice page, (WP) if it is a Word Wroblem page or (DA) if it is a Data Analysis page.

Once you find the page you wish to use, click on the in front of the title.
You may return to this page by clicking on the which appears at the bottom of each page.
