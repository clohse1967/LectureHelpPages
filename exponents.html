Predicting Exponents

This page presents problems involving predicting exponents for basic operations involving numbers in presented in proper scientific notation. When you press "New Problem" either a multiplication or division problem involving two numbers in scientific notation will be displayed to the left of the table. In the answer cell, enter the value of the exponent that results if the operation is carried out and the result expressed in proper scientific notation. Then, press "Check Answer."
  • If you miss a problem three times, pressing "Show Answer" will display the complete solution and you will no longer be able submit an answer for that problem.
