Predicting Exponents

This page allows you to practice predicting exponents for basic operations involving numbers in scientific notation.
When you press "New Problem" a popup will appear with either a multiplication or division problem.
In the cell, enter the value of the exponent that results if the operation is carried out and the result expressed in proper scientific notation. Then, press "Check Answer." Results appear on the main page.

When the small window appears for the first time, you maydrag it to a convenient place on your screen.

Do not use a calculator on this page or you will defeat its purpose.
